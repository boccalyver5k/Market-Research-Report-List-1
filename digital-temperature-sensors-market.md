<p><p>The digital temperature sensor market is categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact sensors. Contact type sensors measure temperature through direct contact with the object, often providing precise readings in applications like HVAC and industrial processes. Non-contact sensors, on the other hand, detect temperature from a distance using infrared technology, making them ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. Both types find extensive use across various sectors, including healthcare, automotive, and consumer electronics, enhancing measurement accuracy and safety.</p></p>
