<p><ul><li>Allergen Screening</li><li>Allergen Classification Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Allergy diagnostic assay kits encompass two primary market types: allergen screening and allergen classification tests. Allergen screening kits are designed for the initial identification of allergen sensitivities by detecting specific IgE antibodies in a patient's blood or through skin tests. In contrast, allergen classification tests provide a more detailed analysis by categorizing identified allergens, allowing for targeted management and treatment plans. Together, these kits facilitate accurate diagnosis and enhance patient care in allergy management, driving growth in the diagnostic market.</p></p>
