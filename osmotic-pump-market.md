<p><strong>The Osmotic Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Implanted Osmotic Pump</li><li>Push-Pull Osmotic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The osmotic pump market features two primary types: implanted osmotic pumps and push-pull osmotic pumps. Implanted osmotic pumps are designed for long-term drug delivery directly into the bloodstream, offering controlled release while minimizing patient intervention. In contrast, push-pull osmotic pumps utilize a mechanism that creates pressure differentials to expel drug solutions when triggered, allowing for more immediate dosing. Both types are essential in enhancing therapeutic efficacy, improving patient adherence, and providing targeted treatment in various medical applications.</p></p>
