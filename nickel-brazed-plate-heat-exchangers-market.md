<p><p>Nickel brazed plate heat exchangers (NBPHEs) are advanced thermal management devices designed for efficient heat transfer in various applications. Due to their compact design and high thermal efficiency, they are increasingly favored in industries such as refrigeration, HVAC, and chemical processing.</p><p>The Nickel Brazed Plate Heat Exchangers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.9% during the forecast period.
