<p><p>The weighbridge market consists primarily of two types: analog and digital weighbridges. Analog weighbridges use mechanical systems with dial gauges to display weight readings, offering simplicity and reliability for basic weighing needs. In contrast, digital weighbridges utilize electronic sensors for precise weight measurement, displaying results on digital screens. They often feature advanced capabilities, such as data connectivity, enhanced accuracy, and user-friendly interfaces, making them suitable for modern applications in various industries, including logistics, construction, and agriculture.</p></p>
