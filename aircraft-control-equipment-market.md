<p><ul><li>Mechanical System</li><li>Hydro-mechanical System</li><li>Fly-by-wire Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Control Equipment market includes three main types: Mechanical Systems, Hydro-mechanical Systems, and Fly-by-Wire Control Systems. </p><p>Mechanical Systems rely on traditional mechanical linkages for control input, ensuring direct pilot feedback. Hydro-mechanical Systems combine hydraulic actuation with mechanical elements, offering enhanced control precision and response. Fly-by-Wire Control Systems utilize electronic signals to replace conventional controls, improving safety and allowing for advanced automation features. Each system plays a crucial role in aviation safety, efficiency, and performance across various aircraft types.</p></p>
