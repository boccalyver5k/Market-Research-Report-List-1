<p><p>The gas isotope analyzers market is divided into two main types: radioisotope analysis and stable isotope analysis. Radioisotope analysis focuses on detecting radioactive isotopes in gas samples, often for applications in environmental monitoring, nuclear industry, and healthcare. In contrast, stable isotope analysis examines non-radioactive isotopes to understand various processes, including climate change, food authenticity, and metabolic studies. Both types serve crucial roles in research and industry, providing insights into elemental composition and isotopic variations in gases.</p></p>
