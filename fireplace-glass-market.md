<p><p>The fireplace glass market is primarily categorized into two types: ceramic glass and tempered glass. Ceramic glass is known for its high heat resistance and ability to withstand direct flames, making it ideal for wood-burning and gas fireplaces. Tempered glass, on the other hand, is engineered to endure thermal stresses, often used in modern designs for its strength and safety features. Both types enhance the aesthetic appeal and functionality of fireplaces while ensuring durability and efficient heat retention.</p></p>
