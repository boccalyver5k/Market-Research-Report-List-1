<p><strong>The Video Pico Projectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCOS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video pico projectors, compact devices for portable projection, primarily utilize two types of technologies: DLP (Digital Light Processing) and LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors use micro-mirrors to create images, offering high brightness and vivid color, making them suitable for well-lit environments. In contrast, LCoS projectors employ liquid crystal panels, delivering superior resolution and color accuracy, which makes them ideal for detailed graphics and presentations. Each technology caters to different user needs within the growing pico projector market.</p></p>
