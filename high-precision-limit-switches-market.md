<p><ul><li>Single Pole Double Throw Switch</li><li>Double Pole Double Throw Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-precision limit switches are critical components in automation, ensuring accurate control and positioning. The Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) switch allows for a single input to control two output paths, making it versatile in applications requiring a changeover function. In contrast, the Double Pole Double Throw (DPDT) switch offers two separate circuits, enabling control of two different devices or processes simultaneously. Both types enhance operational flexibility, improve safety, and provide precise control in industrial applications.</p></p>
