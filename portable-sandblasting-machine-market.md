<p><p>The portable sandblasting machine market is segmented into dry and wet types. Dry sandblasting machines utilize compressed air to propel abrasive materials, making them ideal for removing rust and paint from surfaces quickly and efficiently. In contrast, wet sandblasting machines mix water with abrasives, reducing airborne dust and increasing safety during operation. Wet methods are often preferred for delicate surfaces or environments where dust control is crucial. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing surface preparation and finishing.</p></p>
