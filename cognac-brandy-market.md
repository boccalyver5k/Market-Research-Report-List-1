<p><p>Cognac brandy is categorized into several quality segments based on aging. VS, or "Very Special," is aged for a minimum of two years, offering fresh and fruity notes. VSOP, or "Very Superior Old Pale," requires at least four years of aging, resulting in a smoother, richer flavor profile. XO, or "Extra Old," is aged for a minimum of ten years, delivering a complex and refined taste with deeper, more sophisticated characteristics. Each type reflects the craftsmanship and aging process of the distillers.</p></p>
