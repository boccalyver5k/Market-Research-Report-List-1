<p><p>The industrial rotary valves market comprises various types, including butterfly valves, ball valves, and plug valves. Butterfly valves have a disc that rotates to regulate flow, offering quick operation and low pressure drop. Ball valves feature a spherical disc that provides tight sealing and is ideal for on/off applications. Plug valves utilize a cylindrical or tapered plug that can rotate within the body, allowing for precise flow control. Each type is used across industries for efficient fluid management and process control.</p></p>
