<p><p>The rubber-based flexible foam market is categorized into several types, including Nitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR), Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer (EPDM), and Chloroprene, among others. NBR-based foams are known for their excellent oil and chemical resistance, making them suitable for automotive applications. EPDM is valued for its weather and ozone resistance, ideal for outdoor use. Chloroprene, or Neoprene, offers superior thermal properties and is often used in insulation and protective gear. Other formulations include specialty blends for diverse applications.</p></p>
