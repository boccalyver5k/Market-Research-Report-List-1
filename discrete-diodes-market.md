<p><strong>The Discrete Diodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Schottky diode</li><li>Transient Suppression Diode (TVS)</li><li>Commutation Diode</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The discrete diodes market comprises several key types, each serving unique functions. Schottky diodes are known for their fast switching and low forward voltage drop, making them ideal for power applications. Transient Suppression Diodes (TVS) protect circuits from voltage spikes, ensuring reliability. Commutation diodes are used in switching applications to prevent reverse voltage damage. Other diodes include Zener diodes for voltage regulation and rectifier diodes for converting AC to DC, collectively catering to various electronic needs across industries.</p></p>
