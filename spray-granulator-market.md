<p><p>A spray granulator is a specialized piece of equipment used in the manufacturing process to produce granules from various materials. It operates by spraying a liquid binder onto a powder bed, promoting the agglomeration of particles into larger granules. This process is essential in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food, and chemicals, where uniform particle size and improved flow characteristics are crucial for product performance.</p><p>The Spray Granulator Market is poised for significant growth, expecting a CAGR of 14.1% during the forecast period.
