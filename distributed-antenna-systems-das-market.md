<p><strong>The Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active DAS</li><li>Passive DAS</li><li>Hybrid DAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Distributed Antenna Systems (DAS) enhance wireless communication by distributing signals across multiple antennas throughout a building or area. There are three main market types: </p><p>1. **Active DAS** utilizes powered antennas connected to a centralized base station, providing enhanced coverage and capacity. </p><p>2. **Passive DAS** relies on coaxial cables and splitters that distribute signals without amplification, making it simpler but less flexible. </p><p>3. **Hybrid DAS** combines both active and passive components, offering a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness for varying environments.</p></p>
