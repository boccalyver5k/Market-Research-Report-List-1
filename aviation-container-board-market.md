<p><p>The Aviation Container Board market is categorized mainly into two types: Main Deck Pallet and Lower Deck Pallet. Main Deck Pallets are designed for larger cargo areas in the upper section of cargo aircraft, accommodating bulkier and heavier items, optimizing space and handling efficiency. In contrast, Lower Deck Pallets are intended for the aircraft's lower cargo hold, focusing on smaller, more compact cargo loads. Together, these pallets streamline the logistics of air freight by maximizing space utilization and ensuring secure transport.</p></p>
