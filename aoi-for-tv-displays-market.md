<p><ul><li>Inline AOI Equipment</li><li>Offline AOI Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) for TV displays is categorized into inline and offline AOI equipment. Inline AOI operates continuously within the production line, ensuring real-time quality control by inspecting displays as they are manufactured. This method enhances efficiency and reduces defects promptly. In contrast, offline AOI equipment functions separately from the production line, allowing for batch inspections after manufacturing. This setup is flexible and particularly useful for detailed analysis and rework of specific displays, ensuring high-quality standards.</p></p>
