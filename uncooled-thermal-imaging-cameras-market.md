<p><ul><li>Short Wave IR Cameras</li><li>Mid Wave IR Cameras</li><li>Long Wave IR Cameras</li><li>Far Wave IR Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled thermal imaging cameras market consists of various types based on infrared wavelength ranges. Short Wave IR cameras capture wavelengths from 0.9 to 2.5 micrometers, ideal for specific applications like surveillance. Mid Wave IR cameras, operating between 3 to 5 micrometers, excel in environmental monitoring. Long Wave IR cameras, ranging from 8 to 14 micrometers, are popular in industrial and military settings. Far Wave IR cameras extend beyond 14 micrometers, primarily utilized in specialized research and medical applications.</p></p>
