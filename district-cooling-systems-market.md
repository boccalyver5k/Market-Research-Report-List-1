<p><strong>The District Cooling Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Free Cooling</li><li>Absorption Cooling</li><li>Electric Chillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>District Cooling Systems utilize various types of cooling methods to efficiently provide chilled water for air conditioning in buildings. Free Cooling leverages ambient temperatures, using outside air or water to cool without energy-consuming processes. Absorption Cooling employs heat from sources like natural gas or waste heat to drive the cooling process instead of electricity. Electric Chillers, the most common type, use electricity to compress refrigerants, producing chilled water. Each method offers unique benefits to enhance energy efficiency and environmentally friendly cooling solutions.</p></p>
