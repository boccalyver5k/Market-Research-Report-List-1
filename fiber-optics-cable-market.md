<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode Fiber Optics Cable</li><li>Single-Mode Fiber Optics Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optics cable market consists of two main types: multi-mode and single-mode fiber optics cables. Multi-mode fiber optics cables are designed for short-distance communication, allowing multiple light modes to transmit data simultaneously, making them suitable for local area networks. In contrast, single-mode fiber optics cables are optimized for long-distance transmission, using a single light mode for minimal signal loss and higher bandwidth, making them ideal for wide-area networks and telecommunications. Both types cater to diverse connectivity needs.</p></p>
