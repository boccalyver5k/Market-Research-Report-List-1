<p><p>The carton closing staplers market is categorized into three main types: Manual, Pneumatic, and Electric. Pneumatic staplers utilize compressed air for faster and more efficient stapling, ideal for medium to large-scale production environments. Electric carton closing staplers are powered by electricity, providing high-speed stapling with minimal user effort, suited for high-volume settings where efficiency and consistency are crucial.</p></p>
