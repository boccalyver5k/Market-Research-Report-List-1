<p><ul><li>Fluorescent Lamps</li><li>High-Intensity Discharge (HID) Lights</li><li>LED Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The horticulture lighting market primarily consists of three key types: fluorescent lamps, high-intensity discharge (HID) lights, and LED lights. Fluorescent lamps are energy-efficient and suitable for seedlings and small plants due to their low heat output. HID lights, including metal halide and high-pressure sodium lamps, provide intense light ideal for larger crops but consume more energy and generate more heat. LED lights are becoming increasingly popular due to their longevity, low energy consumption, and customizable light spectra, enhancing plant growth efficiency.</p></p>
