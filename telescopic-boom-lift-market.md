<p><p>The telescopic boom lift market is primarily categorized into two types: truck-mounted and self-propelled lifts. Truck-mounted boom lifts are attached to vehicles, offering mobility and quick deployment for various outdoor tasks, ideal for construction and maintenance. On the other hand, self-propelled boom lifts are independent machines equipped with wheels or tracks, allowing for greater maneuverability and versatility in tight spaces. Both types provide elevated access for workers and equipment but differ in portability and operational scope.</p></p>
