<p><p>The single screw food extruder market is divided into two main types: cold extrusion and hot extrusion. Cold extrusion involves processing ingredients at ambient temperatures, ideal for creating products like pasta and snacks while preserving nutritional quality. In contrast, hot extrusion employs elevated temperatures and pressures to cook and shape materials, commonly used for producing expanded snacks and pet food. Both methods are integral to modern food manufacturing, enhancing texture, flavor, and overall product appeal.</p></p>
