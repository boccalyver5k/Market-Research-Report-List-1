<p><p>The fire-proof hydraulic fluid market is categorized into four main types: HFA, HFB, HFC, and HFD. HFA fluids are water-containing solutions that offer good fire resistance. HFB fluids are oil-based but contain additives for enhanced safety. HFC fluids are water-based synthetic fluids with temperature stability and improved fire resistance. HFD fluids are high-temperature resistant fluids that maintain properties under extreme conditions. Each type is designed to mitigate fire risks in hydraulic systems across various industrial applications.</p></p>
