<p><strong>The Dialysis Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis Equipment</li><li>Peritoneal Dialysis Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dialysis equipment market comprises two main types: hemodialysis equipment and peritoneal dialysis equipment. Hemodialysis equipment is used to filter waste and excess fluids from the blood using a machine and dialyzer, often in a clinical setting. In contrast, peritoneal dialysis equipment utilizes the lining of the abdomen (the peritoneum) as a filter to clear waste, with patients typically performing this procedure at home. Both technologies cater to patients with kidney failure, ensuring effective waste removal.</p></p>
