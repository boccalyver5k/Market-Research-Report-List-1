<p><p>The patrol ship market is divided into two main types: maritime patrol ships and river patrol ships. Maritime patrol ships are designed for operations in open sea environments, focusing on tasks like surveillance, anti-piracy, and search and rescue missions. They are often equipped with advanced technology for long-duration missions. In contrast, river patrol ships operate in inland waters, emphasizing the monitoring of riverine and coastal areas, anti-smuggling efforts, and law enforcement, with a design that allows for navigation in shallow waters.</p></p>
