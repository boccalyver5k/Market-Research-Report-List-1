<p><p>In the Spring Type Safety Valve market, products are categorized into two primary types: closed type and non-closed type. Closed type safety valves provide a sealed environment, preventing fluid loss and ensuring system integrity during operation. They are ideal for applications requiring minimal leakage. Conversely, non-closed type safety valves allow for controlled discharge of pressure, making them suitable for systems where some fluid release is acceptable. Each type addresses specific industry needs, ensuring safety and performance in fluid management systems.</p></p>
