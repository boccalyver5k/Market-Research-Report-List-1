<p><p>The melting furnaces market encompasses several types crucial for metal processing. Cupola furnaces are traditional units for cast iron production, using layered charges to achieve high temperatures. Induction furnaces utilize electromagnetic induction to heat metals efficiently and are favored for their energy efficiency and control over the melting process. Open hearth furnaces, once prominent for steel production, now see limited use but are known for their ability to handle large batches. Each type serves distinct industrial needs depending on application and material.</p></p>
