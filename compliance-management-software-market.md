<p><p>Compliance Management Software (CMS) is available in two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise CMS is installed locally on a company's servers and offers greater control over data security and customization, but requires significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based CMS is hosted on external servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and easier access from anywhere. This model typically involves subscription pricing, reducing initial costs and IT demands while enabling automatic updates and compliance with evolving regulations.</p></p>
