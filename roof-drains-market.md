<p><strong>The Roof Drains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sill Type</li><li>Parapet Type</li><li>Flow Control Type</li><li>Overflow Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roof drains market features several types designed to manage water runoff efficiently. Sill type drains are integrated into the roof edge, allowing for seamless drainage. Parapet type drains are mounted on building parapets, directing water away from the roof surface. Flow control drains feature mechanisms to regulate water flow, preventing pooling. Overflow drains provide an emergency outlet to manage excess water during heavy rainfall. Other types may include specialized designs catering to unique architectural needs or specific environmental conditions.</p></p>
