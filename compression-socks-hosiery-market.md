<p><p>The compression socks and hosiery market comprises two primary types: compression socks and compression hosiery. Compression socks are designed to provide graduated pressure from the ankle upwards, enhancing blood circulation and reducing swelling, making them ideal for athletes and individuals with medical conditions. Compression hosiery, on the other hand, includes knee-high or thigh-high options that offer similar benefits for a larger surface area, often blending functionality with style for everyday wear. Both types aim to improve venous health and comfort.</p></p>
