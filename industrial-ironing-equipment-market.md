<p><p>The industrial ironing equipment market is categorized into fully automatic and semi-automatic types. Fully automatic machines operate independently, handling the entire ironing process with minimal human intervention, leading to higher efficiency and consistent results. In contrast, semi-automatic machines require operator involvement for certain tasks, offering a balance between automation and manual control. These two categories cater to different operational needs, with fully automatic equipment ideal for high-volume settings, while semi-automatic solutions appeal to smaller operations or those seeking flexibility.</p></p>
