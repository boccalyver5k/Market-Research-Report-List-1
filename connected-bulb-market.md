<p><p>Connected bulb markets utilize various wireless communication technologies like ZigBee, Wi-Fi, and Bluetooth to enhance user experience and smart home integration. ZigBee offers low power consumption and mesh networking, making it ideal for large installations. Wi-Fi provides high data throughput and direct connectivity to the internet, facilitating advanced features. In contrast, Bluetooth is suited for short-range control, allowing easy setup and simple pairing. Each technology caters to different user needs, balancing convenience, range, and energy efficiency in smart lighting solutions.</p></p>
