<p><ul><li>OCA</li><li>OCR</li><li>SCA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full-lamination Optical Film market encompasses three main types: Optical Clear Adhesive (OCA), Optical Clear Resin (OCR), and Surface Coating Adhesive (SCA). OCA is used for bonding display components, enhancing clarity and durability. OCR provides optical properties while optimizing thickness and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications. SCA involves surface treatments that improve optical performance and protection. Together, these films enhance the functionality and visual quality of displays in devices such as smartphones, tablets, and televisions.</p></p>
