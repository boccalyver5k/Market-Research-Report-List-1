<p><p>Multi-Chip Modules (MCM) are advanced packaging technologies that integrate multiple integrated circuits (ICs) into a single module, enhancing performance and reducing space. The MCM-L market focuses on low-cost applications, typically using organic substrates, while MCM-D, or distributed modules, employ a combination of different ICs for high-performance applications, often using ceramic substrates. MCM-C combines both MCM-L and MCM-D traits, targeting specialized applications requiring high reliability and performance, often found in aerospace and defense sectors.</p></p>
