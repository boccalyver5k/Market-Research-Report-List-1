<p><p>Blown film extrusion lines are categorized by the number of layers they produce, impacting the film's properties and applications. Three-layer systems offer basic barrier and structural attributes, suitable for packaging. Five-layer lines enhance performance, providing better moisture and gas barriers, ideal for more demanding applications. Seven-layer systems deliver superior flexibility, strength, and multilayer functionality, catering to specialized uses like food packaging and medical applications. Each type addresses specific performance requirements and material efficiencies in the packaging industry.</p></p>
