<p><p>Static Data Masking (SDM) is a technique used to protect sensitive data in non-production environments by creating a copy of the original data where identifiable information is replaced with fictitious data. This ensures that organizations can utilize realistic data for testing and development without exposing sensitive customer or company information. SDM is gaining traction as data privacy regulations tighten globally, compelling businesses to enhance their data protection strategies.</p><p>The Static Data Masking Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 15.00% during the forecast period.
