<p><p>The marking films market is segmented into three main types: PE (Polyethylene) marking films, PP (Polypropylene) marking films, and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) marking films. PE marking films are known for their flexibility and resistance to moisture, making them ideal for outdoor applications. PP marking films offer excellent durability and chemical resistance, suitable for industrial use. PVC marking films are versatile, providing good adhesion and printability, widely used in both commercial and industrial labeling applications.</p></p>
