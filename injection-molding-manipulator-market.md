<p><strong>The Injection Molding Manipulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Axis</li><li>Multi Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Injection Molding Manipulator market is divided into two main types: Single Axis and Multi Axis manipulators. Single Axis manipulators are designed to move along one linear path, making them ideal for simple, repetitive tasks such as part extraction from molds. In contrast, Multi Axis manipulators can move along multiple axes, providing greater flexibility and precision. They are suitable for more complex operations, enabling tasks like rotating, tilting, and positioning items in various orientations, enhancing productivity and efficiency in injection molding processes.</p></p>
