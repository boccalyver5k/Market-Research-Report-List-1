<p><ul><li>Media Filtration Equipment</li><li>Membrane Filtration Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wastewater and water treatment equipment market includes media filtration and membrane filtration equipment, each serving distinct functions. Media filtration equipment utilizes granular materials like sand and gravel to remove suspended solids and contaminants from water, offering a cost-effective solution for pre-treatment processes. Conversely, membrane filtration equipment employs semi-permeable membranes to separate impurities at a molecular level, providing highly efficient purification for various applications. Both types contribute to enhancing water quality and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
