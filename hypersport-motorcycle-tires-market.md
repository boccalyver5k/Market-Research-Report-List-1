<p><p>The hypersport motorcycle tires market is primarily divided into two types: radial and bias tires. Radial tires feature cords that run radially from the center, offering superior grip, stability, and performance at high speeds, making them ideal for racing and sport riding. In contrast, bias tires have a construction where the layers run diagonally, providing a firmer ride and more durability, often preferred for touring or cruiser motorcycles. Both types serve specific performance needs and rider preferences in the market.</p></p>
