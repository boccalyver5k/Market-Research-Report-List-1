<p><p>The Infectious Coryza Vaccine market comprises three primary types: monovalent, bivalent, and trivalent vaccines. Monovalent vaccines target a single strain of the pathogen, providing focused protection. Bivalent vaccines offer immunity against two strains, enhancing effectiveness in diverse environments. Trivalent vaccines incorporate three strains, delivering broad-spectrum protection suitable for regions with multiple circulating pathogens. Each type serves specific epidemiological needs, allowing poultry farmers to select appropriate vaccines based on the prevalent strains in their flocks and geographic areas.</p></p>
