<p><p>Electric scooter rentals operate in two main market types: dockless and station-based. Dockless systems allow users to pick up and drop off scooters anywhere within a designated area, promoting flexibility and convenience. In contrast, station-based models require users to rent scooters from specific docking stations and return them to the same or another station, ensuring organized parking. Each market type caters to different user preferences, with dockless catering to spontaneous trips and station-based systems offering more structure and control.</p></p>
