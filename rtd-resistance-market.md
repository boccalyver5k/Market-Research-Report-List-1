<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The RTD Resistance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-wire RTD</li><li>Three-wire RTD</li><li>Four-wire RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RTD resistance market is segmented into three main types: Two-wire, Three-wire, and Four-wire RTDs. Two-wire RTDs are cost-effective and suitable for short-distance applications, but they can suffer from measurement errors due to lead resistance. Three-wire RTDs improve accuracy by compensating for lead resistance and are commonly used in industrial settings. Four-wire RTDs offer the highest precision by eliminating any impact of lead resistance, making them ideal for laboratory and critical applications. Each type serves specific needs based on cost and accuracy requirements.</p></p>
