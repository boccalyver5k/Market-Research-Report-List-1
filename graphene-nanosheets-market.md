<p><p>The graphene nanosheets market is bifurcated into hydrophilic and hydrophobic types, each serving distinct applications. Hydrophilic graphene nanosheets attract water, enhancing their use in biomedical applications, sensors, and environmental remediation, where interaction with water is vital. In contrast, hydrophobic graphene nanosheets repel water, making them suitable for applications in coatings, electronics, and energy storage, where moisture resistance is crucial.
