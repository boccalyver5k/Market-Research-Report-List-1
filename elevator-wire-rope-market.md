<p><strong>The Elevator Wire Rope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiber Core</li><li>Steel Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elevator wire rope market is primarily divided into two types based on their core material: fiber core and steel core. Fiber core ropes, made with natural or synthetic fibers, offer flexibility and shock absorption, making them suitable for low to moderate loads. Steel core ropes, on the other hand, provide superior strength and durability, making them ideal for high-load applications and harsh environments. Each type is chosen based on the specific requirements of the elevator system and operational conditions.</p></p>
