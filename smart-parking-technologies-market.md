<p><p>The Smart Parking Technologies market offers various sensing solutions to enhance parking management. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to detect the presence of vehicles in parking spots, providing real-time occupancy data. Radar sensors utilize radio waves to measure distance and detect vehicle movement, offering reliable tracking. Image sensors employ cameras and computer vision algorithms to analyze parking spaces visually, enabling smart monitoring and enforcement. Together, these technologies facilitate efficient space utilization, reduce congestion, and improve user experience in urban environments.</p></p>
