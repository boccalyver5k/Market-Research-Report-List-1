<p><p>The Point-of-Use Water Purifiers market comprises three main types: Gravity Purifiers, UV Purifiers, and RO Purifiers. Gravity Purifiers use sedimentation and filtration to remove impurities without electricity, making them cost-effective and user-friendly. UV Purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to eliminate microorganisms, ensuring safe drinking water. RO Purifiers employ reverse osmosis technology to filter out dissolved solids, providing comprehensive purification for chemically contaminated water. Each type addresses specific water quality needs, catering to diverse consumer preferences and requirements.</p></p>
