<p><ul><li>Physical Separation (Mechanical Methods, etc.)</li><li>Chemical Separation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Oil Water Separator market comprises two primary types: physical and chemical separation methods. Physical separation relies on mechanical techniques, such as gravity separation, filtration, and flotation, to separate oils from water based on density differences. Chemical separation involves the use of coagulants and flocculants that facilitate the bonding of oil droplets, allowing them to be removed more effectively. Both methods are essential for treating industrial wastewater and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
