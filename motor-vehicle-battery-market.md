<p><p>The motor vehicle battery market comprises primarily two types: maintenance-free batteries and conventional batteries. Maintenance-free batteries, often used in modern vehicles, require no regular upkeep, utilizing advanced technology to minimize water loss and maximize efficiency. In contrast, conventional batteries require periodic maintenance, such as electrolyte level checks and topping up with distilled water. While maintenance-free options are increasingly popular due to their convenience and reliability, conventional batteries remain in use for their lower initial costs and familiarity among drivers.</p></p>
